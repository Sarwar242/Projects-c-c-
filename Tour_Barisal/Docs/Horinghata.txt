 ******How to go::
    Located south of Barisal, Barguna Rakhain area is connected both by air and road from Dhaka, Chittagong and Barisal.
    Flights are available from Chittagong and Dhaka on United Airways and Regent Airways. Flights tend to be daily during the high season (Oct - Apr) but generally drop back to 3-4 flights per week during the summer and Monsoon (May - Sept).
    The private bus companies have offices near Hotel Sea Queen on the main road, and also down in the Hotel Motel Zone.
